ALGO

ALGO means SOMETHING in Spanish. My Algo means that each person reaching out to me wishes to achieve something different. There are as many different needs and goals as people. I want to help you accomplish your Algo, whether it is learning
a language, translating a text or preparing for a speech. Regardless of the language with which you need help, I will support you in your Algo.

Language Guide

I could use various words to describe what I do –
a teacher, a translator, perhaps even a coach? I have decided to call myself a language guide. Guide – because I focus my efforts on supporting you in accomplishing your language goal. I guide you through the learning process, show you the way and possibilities of self-study and, in case of translation, I help you understand what is now non-understandable. I wish to be your language guide, however you decide on which journey we are setting off, at what pace and how we are travelling. In other words, you determine your Algo and I guide you towards it.
