Courses and translation offer

As the Language Guide I can offer you the following language journeys:

Business class

Do you have an idea for our collaboration? Write to me! I can conduct an in-company course, a workshop or organise a language team-building event. I am open to new forms of cooperation.

Short getaway

Do you want to meet up for a one-on-one class, prepare for a job interview, check your level or have a difficult grammar structure explained? Sign up for a 60-min online class. The price is €30. It is possible to buy a pack of classes after such a meeting.

Long-distance trip

Packs of classes are individual offers of larger number of classes. Therefore, in case you want to receive yours, please fill in the contact form.

Package holiday

If you are a fan of studying in groups – this offer is for you. Write to me to be up-to-date with the current group classes offer.

Translingual voyage

Do you need translation to or from Polish, English or Spanish? You are at the right place. I quote each translation individually, therefore send me a message please.

Exotic journey

If you are looking for a teacher or translator of a different language or a teacher for a child or teenager, contact me and I will find a suitable language specialist for you.

Unlimited travel

This is an extra-ordinary offer for deaf people – online classes by means of a chat. They concentrate on written communication in English or Spanish and boosting reading skills. I will answer detailed questions via email.

Tandem tour

Do you wish to practise your language skills with another person, but you do not know anyone at your level? I will try to find the right language partner for you.
